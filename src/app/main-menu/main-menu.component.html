To create a complete concept map:

In theory, a concept map should have 5 or 6 keywords, and each of them should have around 3 related concepts, having a total of between 18 and 20 concepts.

It is also important to highlight the number of concepts at a congnitive level. To improve the long-term concept retention, it is recommended to have 3 or 4 keywords. However, this can be improved through conceptual recollection, leaving to remember 7 long-term concepts.

In sum, it is recommended to elaborate a concept map using between 5 and 7 keywords and between 15 and 20 concepts in total.
